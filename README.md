Box Out 



Box Out is an app for those whoe are into the reselling game. Those who buy and resell shoes, and clothes. Alot of resellers have the struggle of trying to keep track of what they have bought. Tring to keep track of a few items may be managable, but 50 plus items is just too many to keep track of on a piece of paper. 

My Application erases all those struggles. With my application, you have the ability to keep everything in one place. Keep all your items safe by creating an account, adding anything from single pairs of shoes, to 10 + shoes. this also applies to clothes.

I hope you enjoy your time navigating through my application!
